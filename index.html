ABOUT
Welcome to our company Penta Sky Firma. Pentire is our tyre department that have been supplying tyres for over 10 years and are one of the leading independent tyre wholesalers in the TR. If you’re looking to buy tyres at trade prices then you’ve come to the right place.

We have tires in stock and work closely with carefully selected brands across the world to provide the trade with access to serve our client with any requirment.

As an independent tire wholesaler, we can supply you with the widest range of tyres possible without restriction. Whether you’re looking for all-season tires, low priced run-flats or caravan tires our experienced sales team will be able to help. Our trade customers can order tyres 24 hours a day for same day/next day delivery.

Pentire has a dedicated capital to purchase in significant volumes and pass the savings on to our trading partners. On a case-by-case basis we also offer financing and terms to our partners. Our team is based across multiple time zones, enabling us to respond to opportunities 24/7.
